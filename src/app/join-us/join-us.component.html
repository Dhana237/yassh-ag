Pharmacists

Pharmacy Technicians

Job Desription

Role: Assist pharmacists, manage inventory, and handle customer transactions..

Qualifications: Certified pharmacy technician (CPhT) or equivalent training.

Skills: Organizational skills, multitasking, and knowledge of pharmacy software.

Customer Service Representatives

Job Desription

Role: Handle customer inquiries, manage phone calls, and provide front-desk support.

Qualifications: High school diploma or equivalent, experience in customer service.

Skills: Communication, patience, and problem-solving.

Delivery Drivers

Job Desription

Role: Deliver prescriptions and over-the-counter products to customers..

Qualifications: Valid driver’s license, knowledge of local routes.

Skills: Time management, customer service, and reliability.

IT

Qualifications: Background in IT or e-commerce.

Skills: Technical knowledge, problem-solving, and attention to detail.

Store Assistants

Job Desription

Role: Stock shelves, manage inventory, and assist customers with product selection.

Qualifications: Experience in retail or customer service.

Skills: Customer service, organization, and teamwork.

Marketing and Community Outreach Coordinators

Job Desription

Role: Promote the pharmacy’s services, manage social media, and organize health-related events.

Qualifications: Background in marketing, communications, or public relations.

Clinical Pharmacists

Job Desription

Role: Provide medication therapy management, conduct patient consultations, and collaborate with healthcare providers.

Qualifications: Doctor of Pharmacy (Pharm.D) degree, residency training.

Healthcare Consultants
